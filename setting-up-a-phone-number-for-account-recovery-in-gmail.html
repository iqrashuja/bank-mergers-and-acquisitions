Exploring the Process of Adding a Phone Number for Account Recovery in Gmail

Adding a phone number for account recovery in Gmail is a straightforward process that can greatly enhance the security of your account. To begin, open your Gmail account on a web browser and click on your profile picture or initial in the top right corner. From the drop-down menu, select "Manage your Google Account." This will take you to your account settings page where you can manage various aspects of your Google account, including security.

Next, navigate to the "Security" tab on the left-hand side of the page. Under the "Ways we can verify it's you" section, you will find an option to add a recovery phone number. Click on the "Add a recovery phone" button and follow the prompts to enter your phone number. Once you have entered the number, Gmail will verify it by sending a code either via call or text message. Simply enter the code when prompted, and your phone number will be added for account recovery.
