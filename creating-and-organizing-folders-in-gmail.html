First, navigate to your Gmail inbox and locate the "Create new label" option on the left-hand side of the screen. Click on it, and a new window will open where you can enter the name of your folder. Choose a concise and descriptive label that will help you easily identify the type of emails to be filed in that folder. Once you've created your folder, you can begin organizing your emails by simply dragging and dropping them into the appropriate folder.
