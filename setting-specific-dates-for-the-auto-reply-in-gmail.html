Setting up the autoreply feature in Gmail

Setting up the auto-reply feature in Gmail is a straightforward process that can save you time and keep your contacts informed when you're away. To begin, log in to your Gmail account and click on the gear icon in the upper right corner of the screen. From the drop-down menu, select "Settings." Once you're in the Settings section, navigate to the "General" tab, where you'll find various options to customize your email experience. Scan through the menu until you find the "Vacation responder" section, and click on the radio button next to "Vacation responder on."

After selecting the option to turn on the auto-reply feature, you'll need to specify the dates during which you want the responder to be active. Simply choose the start and end dates by clicking on the respective calendar icons, and a pop-up calendar will appear for your convenience. Gmail allows you to set the auto-reply for a single day or even for an entire month. This flexibility ensures that your auto-reply is activated during the exact time frame you desire.
