A StepbyStep Guide to Setting Up Account Recovery via Security Codes in Gmail

To set up account recovery via security codes in Gmail, follow these simple steps. First, open your Gmail account and click on your profile picture or initial in the top right corner. From the drop-down menu, select "Manage your Google Account." This will take you to the account settings page.

Next, navigate to the "Security" tab on the left-hand side of the screen. Here, you will find various options to enhance the security of your account. Scroll down to the section labeled "Ways we can verify it's you" and click on the "Recovery codes" option.

You will be prompted to enter your password to proceed. Once you've done so, click on the "Set up" button. Gmail will then provide you with a list of security codes that you can download and save for future use. It is recommended to keep a backup copy of these codes in a secure location. With these security codes in place, you can easily recover your Gmail account in case of a forgotten password or a compromised account.
