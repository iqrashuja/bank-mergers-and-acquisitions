How to Set Up Trusted Devices for Gmail Account Recovery

Setting up trusted devices for Gmail account recovery is a straightforward process that can provide an added layer of security to your account. To begin, log in to your Gmail account and navigate to the "Security" section in your account settings. Here, you will find the option to manage your trusted devices.

Clicking on the "Manage Devices" button will allow you to add and remove trusted devices from your account. To add a new device, simply click on the "Add Device" button and follow the prompts to verify your identity. This typically involves receiving a verification code through text message or email, which you will need to enter to confirm your ownership of the device. Once the device is added, it will be considered a trusted device and will be recognized whenever you need to recover your account.
